About Us

Koncept is Victoria's newest integrated distributor of plumbing and lighting fixtures for the construction industry. We are a locally owned company that offers a range of high quality products in the distinct categories of kitchen, bath and lighting. We are a group of experienced individuals that have been a part of the building industry for several years. Our vision is to supply all builders, homeowners, designers and trades with superior and high quality products over the industry standard. Our goal is to distribute products at affordable prices and provide a higher standard of living to our clients. We strive on delivering exceptional service and focused on satisfying all customer needs.

Our Location

We are a new modern & sleek showroom offering a nice selection of plumbing fixtures , lighting & electric fireplaces. Our experienced sales consultants are here to help you select fixtures for your next project whether it is a simple faucet or a large development. We have an array of items that are suited for all budgets. Koncept Bath and Lighting is locally owned.
